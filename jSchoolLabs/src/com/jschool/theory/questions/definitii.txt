De scris explicatiile fiecarui termen (in scris ca in ex mai jos) si de explicat diferentele (oral):
1. Declararea, definirea, initializarea, incrementarea si decrementarea, atributirea. (Declaration, definition, initialization, incrementation and decrementation, asign)
	Declaration: You are declaring that something exists, such as a class, function or variable. You don't say anything about what that class or function looks like, you just say that it exists.
	Definition: You define how something is implemented, such as a class, function or variable, i.e. you say what it actually is.
	
2. Bucla (latz), ciclu, iteratie, initializarea, incrementul, conditia de oprire, oprire fortata, continuare (Loop, cycle, iteration, initialization, increment or decrement, stop cycle condition, break, continue).

3. Vector, matrice (tablou), element, pozitie (index), valoare, marime (lungime) (1,2 and N dimensional array, matrix, element, position (index), value, size (lenght))

4. List, ArrayList, LinkedList, Set, Map, TreeSet,
  
5. Variabila, constanta, functie, metoda, procedura, proprietate, atribut, cimp (variable, constant, function, method, procedure, property, attribute, field)

6. Clasa, Interfata, abstract, partial, structure, pattern, object, getter & setter, access level, public, private, protected.

7. Alocare de memorie, static, dynamic, final, constructor, destructor.

8. Parametru de intrare si iesire, argument, variabila, valoare, global, local, void.

9. If/else, for, while, do/while, goto, label, switch/default.

10. Exception, try, catch, finally.
